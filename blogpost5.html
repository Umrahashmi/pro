Different versions of you

By Umra
17 July,2 min read

Isn't it beautiful how different people hold
different versions of me, some know me as
the one who never talks, some know me as
someone who would never shut up, some
know me as the strongest one, and some
know me as an emotional child, some know
me as the least bothered human, and some
know me as the most curious one,some
know me as the camera shy girl, and some
see my personal vlogs because thats what
being a human is all about,
and isn't that beautiful?
